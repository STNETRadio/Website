Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a monthly service fee?
No, the core service is free. Charges may satisfy only if you choose to upgrade to a higher-tier package with advanced features.
Will I earn revenue?
Yes. You can enable monetization across various channels, with earnings directly depending on your podcast's performance and reach.
Can I join if I have another contract?
If you are currently under contract with another network, please wait until your contract expires or is legally terminated before applying to STNET Radio.
Where will my podcast appear?
Your show will appear on global platforms including Apple Podcasts, Amazon Music, Spotify, YouTube Music, and many more—automatically distributed for you.
