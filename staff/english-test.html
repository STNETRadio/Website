About STNET Radio

STNET Radio is an online radio station dedicated to creating creative content and offering a diverse range of topics and broadcast formats to its listeners. Beyond serving its audience, STNET Radio also provides a platform for talented creators and dreamers to develop and share their radio content. We firmly believe that radio communication remains relevant and valuable, offering a convenient, economical and easily accessible channel that fosters a sense of intimacy with our listeners.

About the EF SET

The EF SET is a certifying exam that rests on a foundation of evidence-based research. It was developed to align with the Common European Framework of Reference (CEFR), an internationally recognized standard. The EF SET reliably assesses language skills across the full CEFR range, from pre-A1 to C2.

About EF

EF is an international education company that focuses on language, academics, cultural exchange and educational travel. Founded in 1965, EF’s mission is “opening the world through education.” Millions of students, companies and organizations have participated in an EF program and tens of thousands are currently learning a language with EF.
