1. Information we collect

WHEN YOU APPLY FOR THE PODCASTER ROLE, WE COLLECT THE FOLLOWING CATEGORIES OF INFORMATION:

  • PERSONAL IDENTIFICATION INFORMATION (NAME, NICKNAME, AGE, COUNTRY)
  • CONTACT DETAILS (EMAIL ADDRESS, PHONE NUMBER, SOCIAL MEDIA LINKS)
  • PROFESSIONAL EXPERIENCES (PRIOR PODCASTING/RADIO EXPERIENCE, EDITING SKILLS, EQUIPMENT LIST)
  • CREATIVE CONCEPTS (PODCAST CATEGORIES, SHOW NAME IDEAS, PITCHES, TARGET AUDIENCES)
  • MEDIA (AUDIO/VIDEO SAMPLE LINKS SUBMITTED FOR REVIEW)

2. How we collect information

WE COLLECT INFORMATION DIRECTLY FROM YOU WHEN YOU COMPLETE THE PODCASTER APPLICATION FORM ON OUR WEBSITE.

3. Use of information

WE USE THE COLLECTED INFORMATION EXCLUSIVELY FOR THE FOLLOWING PURPOSES:

  • TO EVALUATE YOUR QUALIFICATIONS AND SUITABILITY FOR THE PODCASTER ROLE.
  • TO REVIEW YOUR CREATIVE IDEAS AND AUDIO/VIDEO SAMPLES.
  • TO COMMUNICATE WITH YOU REGARDING THE STATUS OF YOUR APPLICATION OR TO REQUEST FURTHER DETAILS.

4. Storage of information

INFORMATION FILLED IN THE APPLICATION, BOTH IN PAPER AND ELECTRONIC FORM, WILL BE KEPT IN OUR PARTNER'S CLOUD SYSTEM FOR A PERIOD OF 60 DAYS. WE IMPLEMENT APPROPRIATE TECHNICAL MEASURES TO SAFEGUARD YOUR PERSONAL AND CREATIVE DATA FROM UNAUTHORIZED ACCESS.

5. Data retention

INTERVIEW DATA, AUDIO, VIDEO, AND OTHER INFORMATION WILL BE KEPT IN THE SYSTEM FOR 60 DAYS FOR QUALIFICATION VERIFICATION AND FURTHER QUALITY IMPROVEMENT. AFTER THIS PERIOD, THE DATA WILL BE SECURELY DELETED.

6. Information sharing

WE WILL NEVER SELL YOUR APPLICATION DATA OR PITCHES TO THIRD PARTIES. YOUR INFORMATION IS REVIEWED STRICTLY INTERNALLY BY OUR CONTENT AND RECRUITMENT TEAMS.

7. Your choices

IN RELATION TO YOUR APPLICATION DATA, YOU HAVE THE RIGHT TO:

  • REQUEST ACCESS TO A COPY OF THE INFORMATION YOU SUBMITTED.
  • CORRECT ANY INACCURACIES IN YOUR CONTACT DETAILS.
  • REQUEST THE DELETION OR WITHDRAWAL OF YOUR APPLICATION AND ASSOCIATED DATA AT ANY TIME.

8. Security

WE TAKE REASONABLE MEASURES TO PROTECT YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION FROM UNAUTHORIZED ACCESS, ALTERATION, AND DESTRUCTION. NEVERTHELESS, TRANSFERS OF DATA OVER THE INTERNET CARRY INHERENT PRECAUTIONARY RISKS.

9. Updates to privacy policy

CHANGES TO THIS APPLICATION PRIVACY POLICY WILL BE EFFECTIVE UPON POSTING ON THIS PAGE. APPLICANTS WHOSE DATA IS ALREADY UNDER REVIEW MAY BE NOTIFIED MATERIALLY VIA EMAIL.

10. Contact us

IF YOU HAVE ANY QUESTIONS REGARDING THIS APPLICATION PRIVACY POLICY, PLEASE CONTACT US AT LEGAL@STNETRADIO.COM.
